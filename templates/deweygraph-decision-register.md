## Entry 001

### Rule
Research notebooks are classified by dominant subject or studio function, not automatically under writing.

### Reason
Notebook form does not determine intellectual identity.

### Trigger Pattern
Sketchbooks, electronics notebooks, philosophy journals

### Anchor Guidance
- circuit notebook -> 621.3.CIRC
- poetry notebook -> 821.POET
- studio notebook -> 700.STUDIO

### Link Guidance
Use secondary domains and part-of links when part of a project.

### Organisation Guidance
Keep notebooks with project corpus if context matters.

### Example
A circuit sketchbook belongs under electronics, not writing.
