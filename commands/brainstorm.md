# /brainstorm

Invoke the story-brainstorming skill to develop a new story concept, character, world, or narrative challenge.

**Usage:** `/brainstorm [optional topic or story idea]`

This command triggers `novel-superpowers:story-brainstorming`, which guides you through a structured creative dialogue: exploring existing project context, asking clarifying questions one at a time, proposing multiple story approaches, and producing a complete story bible before any drafting begins.

**When to use:**
- Starting a new novel, story, or major arc
- Developing a new character or subplot
- Exploring a new genre direction
- Resolving a plot or structural problem
