# /draft

Invoke the drafting-scenes skill to execute a story outline by writing chapters and scenes in sequence.

**Usage:** `/draft [optional chapter or scene name]`

This command triggers `novel-superpowers:drafting-scenes`, which loads the current outline, reviews it critically, and writes each scene or chapter with continuity verification before claiming completion.

**When to use:**
- After completing and approving a story outline
- Resuming a draft in progress
- Writing a specific chapter or scene from an existing outline
- Executing a revised outline after story restructuring
