# /outline

Invoke the story-outlining skill to create a detailed chapter-by-chapter and scene-by-scene outline from an approved story bible.

**Usage:** `/outline [optional story or chapter name]`

This command triggers `novel-superpowers:story-outlining`, which takes an approved story bible or premise and produces a structured outline with beat-level detail, character arc tracking, and scene-by-scene breakdowns ready for drafting.

**When to use:**
- After completing story brainstorming and approving the story bible
- Beginning a new act, chapter cluster, or sequel
- Re-outlining after major structural changes
- Creating a fresh outline for a specific subplot or POV thread
