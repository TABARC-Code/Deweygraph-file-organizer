# Outline Reviewer Prompt Template

Use this when you want a subagent to review a completed story outline for bible compliance, scene completeness, continuity consistency, and drafting readiness.

---

## Prompt

You are reviewing a story outline for readiness to draft. You are checking: does this outline cover the story bible's requirements, are the scene entries complete enough to draft from, and are there any internal contradictions or gaps?

### Story Bible

[Paste the story bible — or a summary if the bible is very long]

### Outline to Review

[Paste the complete story outline]

---

### Your Review Tasks

**1. Bible coverage**
Does the outline cover every major story beat promised in the bible? Check:
- All major character arcs have scenes that show the key arc stages (start / turn / end)
- The central conflict is present and escalating through the acts
- All characters named in the bible appear in the outline

**2. Scene completeness**
For each scene entry, are the following present?
- Scene goal (what does the POV character want?)
- Scene conflict (what opposes them?)
- Scene outcome (yes-and / yes-but / no-and / no-but)
- Continuity flags (facts established here)
- Emotional beat

Flag any scenes with missing sections.

**3. Placeholder scan**
Are there any scene entries with: "TBD," "figure out in draft," "something happens here," or other vague content? List them.

**4. Act structure**
Does the outline follow its declared act structure (three-act / beat sheet / kishōtenketsu / etc.)? Are the major turning points (inciting incident, midpoint, all-is-lost, climax) present and clearly labeled?

**5. Internal continuity**
Do any scenes in the outline establish facts that later scenes contradict? Check:
- Character states (a character loses something in Scene 5 — do later scenes account for this?)
- Timeline (is the sequence coherent?)
- Cause and effect (does each scene's outcome make the next scene's starting position credible?)

**6. Pacing**
Are there stretches of 3+ consecutive scenes with the same emotional register? Flag them — pacing requires variation.

---

### Reporting Format

```
OUTLINE REVIEW

Bible coverage: [✅ Full / ❌ Gaps found]
[List missing story beats if any]

Scene completeness: [✅ All complete / ❌ Incomplete entries]
[List scene numbers with missing elements]

Placeholders: [✅ None / ❌ Found]
[List scenes with placeholder content]

Act structure: [✅ Present / ❌ Missing beats]
[Note any missing major turning points]

Internal continuity: [✅ Consistent / ❌ Issues found]
[List specific contradictions]

Pacing: [✅ Varied / ⚠️ Monotony detected]
[List stretches of same emotional register]

VERDICT: [✅ Ready for drafting / ❌ Needs revision before drafting]

REQUIRED REVISIONS: (if any)
1. [Specific scene or section — what's missing or contradicted]
```
